Our Story

Born from a love of nature's quiet moments.

Nature Nest began in a small kitchen studio in Dubai, with one simple belief: that a candle should do more than just burn. It should transport you. Every piece we make — from sculpted wax flowers to hand-poured aromatherapy candles — carries the signature of a real person who cared enough to make it.

We use only pure soy wax, natural beeswax, botanical essential oils, and lead-free cotton wicks. No paraffin. No synthetic fragrances. No shortcuts.
